About Altiora

In Latin, altiora means “higher things” or “deeper things,” reflecting our commitment to helping people pursue meaningful, lasting wellbeing.


Altiora Wellness provides guidance, intervention, mentorship, outreach, advocacy, and consulting services designed to simplify access to services and supports for the people we serve. Our approach is rooted in experience and the belief that every person has the capacity to move forward and make meaningful improvements in their life.


We support individuals and families experiencing addiction, mental health, and life challenges by helping them navigate available resources, identify achievable goals, and develop practical strategies for lasting wellbeing and recovery.

Why Altiora?

With over 17 years of experience in addiction, mental health, crisis intervention, and community support services, I understand how challenging it can be to navigate a complex network of resources and determine what support is the right fit.

At Altiora, I provide a more personal and guided approach. I take the time to meet with you, listen to your concerns, understand your goals, and develop a personalized plan that supports meaningful progress. Together, we will identify practical resources, build on your strengths, and create a path toward wellbeing, resilience, and lasting change.

Meet the Founder

Altiora Wellness Consulting was founded from a passion for supporting individuals and families through some of life's most challenging circumstances. Throughout my career in addiction, mental health, crisis intervention, withdrawal management, shelter services, and community support, I have seen how difficult it can be to navigate complex systems and find the right resources. I created Altiora to provide a more personal and guided approach—helping people identify meaningful goals, access appropriate supports, and feel encouraged throughout the process.


I believe lasting change comes when people are empowered to recognize their strengths, take an active role in their wellbeing, and move forward with confidence. My commitment is to offer practical guidance grounded in experience while helping people build resilience, create positive change, and work toward lasting wellbeing and recovery.

Our Services

Guidance & Mentorship

Navigating the care system shouldn't feel like an uphill battle. I provide the clarity, guidance, and support you need to move forward with confidence.

  • System Navigation: Navigate the often-complex network of addiction, mental health, and community support services with confidence and clarity.
  • Resource Matching: Identify the programs, services, housing options, and community resources that best align with your unique needs and goals.
  • Ongoing Mentorship: Receive compassionate guidance, practical tools, and ongoing support to make informed decisions for yourself or a loved one.

Consulting & Coaching

Whether you are facing a major life transition or actively working toward recovery, you don't have to figure it out alone. Together, we'll create a personalized path forward that reflects your goals, strengths, and unique circumstances.

  • Personalized Goal Setting: Develop a practical, achievable plan tailored to your current priorities, values, and circumstances.
  • Resilience Building: Build effective coping strategies and everyday habits that support long-term wellbeing, confidence, and personal growth.
  • Accountability & Support: Receive ongoing encouragement, compassionate guidance, and accountability to help you stay focused, overcome challenges, and celebrate meaningful progress.

Education & Program Design

Empowering workplaces and communities through wellness, mental health, and recovery education. I create engaging workshops, educational resources, and practical initiatives designed to reflect the unique goals, culture, and needs of your organization or community group.

  • Tailored Workshops: Develop educational presentations, workshops, and training programs customized for your organization, workplace, or community group.
  • Prevention Initiatives: Design practical wellness, addiction, and mental health initiatives that promote prevention, resilience, and recovery.
  • Resource Design: Create customized educational resources and program frameworks that align with your goals, target audience, and organizational environment.
  • Community Engagement: Deliver interactive, evidence-informed education that builds confidence, increases mental health literacy, simplifies complex systems, and supports meaningful, lasting change.
