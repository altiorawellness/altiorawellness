About Altiora

In Latin, altiora means "higher things" or "deeper things," reflecting our commitment to helping people pursue meaningful, lasting wellbeing.

Altiora Wellness provides guidance, mentorship, advocacy, and consulting to help individuals and families navigate addiction, mental health, and community support systems with greater clarity and confidence.

Grounded in over 17 years of frontline experience, we help people access the right resources, identify achievable goals, and develop practical strategies for lasting wellbeing and recovery. We believe everyone has the capacity to move forward with the right support.

Why Altiora?

With over 17 years of experience in addiction, mental health, crisis intervention, and community support services, I understand how challenging it can be to navigate a complex network of resources and determine what support is the right fit.

At Altiora, I provide a more personal and guided approach. I take the time to meet with you, listen to your concerns, understand your goals, and develop a personalized plan that supports meaningful progress. Together, we will identify practical resources, build on your strengths, and create a path toward wellbeing, resilience, and lasting change.

How It Works

Getting started is simple. Altiora provides a clear, supportive path forward.

📞

1 — Start a Conversation

Reach out by email or phone. We’ll take the time to listen, understand your concerns, and discuss what support may be helpful.

🗓️

2 — Meet & Plan

We’ll meet in a comfortable community setting, such as a coffee shop, library, or local park, to discuss your goals and create a practical plan forward.

🌿

3 — Ongoing Guidance

Continue meeting with personalized guidance, accountability, and support as you work toward your goals and lasting wellbeing.

Meet the Founder

I founded Altiora from a passion for supporting individuals and families through some of life's most challenging circumstances. Throughout my career in addictions, mental health, crisis intervention, and community support, I have witnessed how overwhelming it can be to navigate complex systems, understand available options, and find the right resources.


I believe lasting change begins when people recognize their strengths and have the right support along the way. My commitment is to provide practical guidance grounded in real-world experience while helping individuals and families build resilience and create meaningful change.

Important Notice & Crisis Support

Altiora Wellness Consulting provides wellness coaching, system navigation, advocacy, education, and consulting services. We do not provide psychotherapy, clinical diagnoses, or emergency crisis services.

If you or someone you know is experiencing an emergency or is in immediate danger, please call 911 or call or text 988 (Suicide Crisis Helpline), or go to your nearest emergency department.
