About Altiora

In Latin, altiora means “higher things” or “deeper things,” reflecting our commitment to helping individuals and organizations pursue meaningful, lasting wellbeing.


Altiora was founded on over 17 years of experience in addiction, mental health, and community support services. The work is grounded in frontline clinical practice, crisis intervention, and trauma-informed care within high-acuity environments.


Experience includes leadership roles in shelter and community services, withdrawal management, and frontline support across health and social service systems.
