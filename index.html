About Altiora

In Latin, altiora means "higher things" or "deeper things," reflecting our commitment to helping individuals and organizations pursue meaningful, lasting wellbeing. Altiora was founded on over 17 years of experience in addiction, mental health, and community support services. The work is grounded in frontline clinical practice, crisis intervention, and trauma-informed care within high-acuity environments. Experience includes leadership roles in shelter and community services, withdrawal management, and frontline support across health and social service systems. This background has built strong expertise in client-centered care, practical intervention, and supporting individuals through complex health and social challenges. Altiora is based on the belief that effective support must be practical, individualized, and rooted in real-world experience working with individuals, families, and communities.

Our Services

Altiora provides interventions, prevention plans, one-on-one, group, and family supports, as well as referrals. Services are grounded in practical, individualized strategies that support sustainable health outcomes. Each plan is tailored to meet the unique needs of the individual.
